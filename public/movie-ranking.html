Top Movies of all time According to Zoie

My top 3 movies of all-time

Little Rascals

This is my favorite 90's movie, I love the kids acting and humor.

The Adventures of Winnie the Pooh

This is a timeless, heartwarming classic cartoon that will always hold a special place in my heart.

Pacific Rim

Really neat sci-fi monster movie, has some of my favorite fight scenes.
